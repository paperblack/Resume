### Commands to Modify Font Sizes 

| Command          |   10pt  |  11pt  |  12pt |
-----------------------------------------------
| \tiny            |   5      | 6     |  6  |
| \scriptsize      |   7      | 8     |  8  |
| \footnotesize    |   8      | 9     |  10 |
| \small           |   9      | 10    |  10.95 |
| \normalsize      |   10     | 10.95 |  12 |
| \large           |   12     | 12    |  14.4 |
| \Large           |   14.4   | 14.4  |  17.28 |
| \LARGE           |   17.28  | 17.28 |  20.74 |
| \huge            |   20.74  | 20.74 |  24.88 |
| \Huge            |   24.88  | 24.88 |  24.88 |
